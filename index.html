Portfolio

10+ consumer products

7 years across marketplaces, AI apps, creator tools, mobile growth, serverless infrastructure, and real-world operations. Some worked, some didn't — all of them taught something.

Commerce growth

TikTok CPI $0.09, paid CAC $8

Problem: Consumer commerce funnel with thin margins — standard CAC math was lying to us.

Result: $0.09 TikTok CPI and $8 paid CAC per buyer in live commerce experiments — documented unit economics, not vanity metrics.

AI in production

Stable Diffusion API in 2 days

Problem: Competitor shipped an AI image product; we had no backend and two weeks felt too long.

Result: Production API live in 48 hours on serverless AWS. The hard part wasn't the build — it was everything after launch.

Infrastructure

Serverless at founder scale

Problem: Tiny teams can't carry heavy infra across 10+ products.

Result: Repeatable Lambda/DynamoDB/S3 stack from Hypee through cheap.market. I still write the production code myself.
